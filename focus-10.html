01

Energy Conversion Box

ECB

The purpose is to take whatever is on your mind prior to beginning the exercise and transform it into a symbol before putting it in the box. The box itself can take any form meaningful to you.

It’s a place to put all your physical matter worries and concerns — essentially a place to dump your fears and convert them to power.

Note — If you don’t have much of an internal dialogue and can leave daily distractions behind naturally, you may not need this tool much at all.

02

Resonant Tuning

RT

This consciousness tool uses breath and sound to increase, move, and balance the energy flow in your physical and energy bodies. It is an effective way to quiet your mind, enhance your energy levels, and support deep meditative states.

It is a breathing exercise to help you vitalize and charge your entire system. It promotes an accelerated gathering of your vibrational energy while reducing your internal dialogue.

In practice, it involves humming and toning along with voices on the tape, building energy before moving forward.

03

Resonant Energy Balloon

REBAL

You create a flow pattern with your breath — letting energy flow out of the top of your head like a fountain, allowing it to continue flowing gently down and around you, then re-entering your body through the bottoms of your feet. Then shift the flow pattern into a spiral, winding the energy down around you and coiling it back up inside you.

Your REBAL is an intensification of your own energy, helping to create a high-energy state within and around you. Operating on a principle of resonance, it acts like a sieve or filter: only energy that is equal to, or of a higher or more refined vibration than yours will have access to your energy field.

It also acts as a magnet, attracting influences and guidance from higher energy forms.

Note — The original name is REBAL, not “resonance balloon.” The REBAL is a breath-driven energy flow — not a static bubble you visualize around yourself. That is a meaningful difference in practice.

04

The Gateway Affirmation

GA
I am more than my physical body. Because I am more than physical matter, I can perceive that which is greater than the physical world. Therefore, I deeply desire to Expand, to Experience; to Know, to Understand; to Control, to Use such greater energies and energy systems as may be beneficial and constructive to me and to those who follow me. Also, I deeply desire the help and cooperation, the assistance, the understanding of those individuals whose wisdom, development, and experience are equal to or greater than my own. I ask their guidance and protection from any influence or any source that might provide me with less than my stated desires.

Key distinction — The REBAL is a breath-driven energy flow: fountain out the top of the head, spiral back in through the feet. It is not a static bubble visualized around the body. That is a meaningful difference in practice.
