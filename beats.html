Offline Player — Plays With The Screen Off

Wired stereo → Pick a state → Play & lock → Drift

Stereo headphones are non-negotiable — the beat only forms inside your brain when each ear receives its own tone, so use wired headphones (Bluetooth codecs can collapse the channels). This is the offline build: it renders the whole session to an audio file the moment you press Start, so you can lock the screen and it keeps playing — lock-screen play/pause works too, and the battery isn't drained by the display. Never use while driving; consult a physician if you have epilepsy or take psychotropic medication.
